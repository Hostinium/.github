# qless

qless is a service-operations product focused on queue-led environments where booking, throughput, on-site transaction handling, and operational visibility need to work together.

## Public Summary

The product is designed for businesses that need tighter control over customer flow, service execution, and point-of-sale activity without relying on fragmented tooling.

## Suitable Environments

- queue-managed service centers
- service venues with booking and walk-in activity
- operational teams that need throughput visibility
- front-desk and on-site payment workflows

## Public Boundary

This brief is intentionally limited to the business and positioning layer. Internal workflow logic, implementation structure, and delivery-specific configuration remain non-public.
